# AI Agency Name Research & Recommendations

## Executive Summary

After conducting extensive research on AI agency naming trends, domain availability, credibility factors, and naming psychology for 2025, I've identified several strong naming options that balance uniqueness, credibility, and AI focus.

---

## Key Research Findings

### 2025 Naming Trends for AI Agencies

1. **Human-Centered Naming** - Moving away from overly technical jargon toward names that evoke warmth and relatability
2. **One-Word Power Names** - Single-word names like "Perplexity" and "Cohere" convey confidence and scalability
3. **Strategic AI Integration** - Including "AI" directly signals expertise, though some experts suggest letting the name imply technology instead
4. **Clarity Over Cleverness** - Names should be easy to pronounce, spell, and remember
5. **Mission-Driven Identity** - Names that reflect values and outcomes, not just technology

### Domain Extension Strategy

**Best Options for Credibility:**
- **.ai** - Instant industry association, signals AI expertise ($100/year, 2-year minimum)
- **.com** - Gold standard for universal trust and recognition
- **.io** - Tech-focused, popular with startups and SaaS companies
- **.tech** - Direct technology communication
