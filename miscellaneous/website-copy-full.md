**Column 4: Technical Debt Kills Momentum**

That "temporary workaround" from 6 months ago is still there. The database doesn't support new features. Simple changes require touching 12 files because nothing was built to scale. Tasks that should take 2 days take 2 weeks because developers are afraid of breaking something.

The cost compounds exponentially. You're paying $150/hour to untangle messes instead of building revenue-generating features. Eventually, you face the nightmare: rebuild from scratch or keep patching a sinking ship.
