Revive Air — Automated Lead Generation System

AI-powered lead generation system delivering 200+ qualified HVAC leads daily with 95% faster response times and 67% conversion rate

The Challenge

Revive Air, a growing HVAC service company, was struggling with manual lead qualification and slow response times. Their team was spending 15+ hours weekly manually sorting through leads from multiple sources—Google Ads, Facebook, website forms, and phone calls—resulting in missed opportunities and frustrated potential customers.

The main pain points were:

  • Average response time of 4-6 hours (industry standard is under 5 minutes)
  • 35% of leads never received a response during business hours
  • Manual data entry causing errors and duplicate records
  • No lead scoring or prioritization system

The Solution

We designed and implemented a comprehensive AI automation system that handles the entire lead lifecycle—from capture to qualification to CRM integration—with minimal human intervention.
