FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how we work.

What industries do you work with?
We work with SaaS companies, startups, agencies, e-commerce businesses, and B2B service companies across all industries. If you have repetitive processes to automate or need a custom product built, we can help. Our solutions are industry-agnostic and customized to your specific business needs.

Do I own the code you write?
Yes, 100%. You receive full source code access and complete ownership of everything we build. There's no vendor lock-in, no ongoing licensing fees, and you're free to modify, host, or transfer your product anywhere you choose.

What if I need changes after launch?
All projects include 30 days of post-launch support for bug fixes and minor adjustments. For ongoing changes, feature additions, or optimization, we offer flexible retainer packages or project-based pricing depending on your needs.

How do you deliver so fast?
We use AI-assisted development tools (Claude, Cursor, Make.com) combined with productized processes and reusable component libraries. We're not reinventing the wheel for every project—we leverage proven frameworks and modern technology to accelerate delivery without compromising quality.

Can you integrate with my existing tools?
Absolutely. We integrate with CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive), payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), calendars (Google, Outlook), project management tools (Notion, Asana, Monday), and 1,000+ other platforms via APIs and automation tools.

What's your refund policy?
If we don't deliver what we promised within the agreed timeline, you get a full refund. We stand behind our work and our commitments. Your success is our success.
