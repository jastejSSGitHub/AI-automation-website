GrowthTech — Automated Client Onboarding

Intelligent onboarding automation reducing setup time from 5-7 days to 4 hours, enabling 3x client capacity growth

The Challenge

GrowthTech, a digital marketing agency, was hitting a growth ceiling. Their manual client onboarding process was taking 5-7 business days per client, requiring extensive back-and-forth emails, manual data collection, and repetitive account setups across multiple platforms (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, Analytics, CRM).

This bottleneck meant they could only onboard 8-10 new clients per month, and their team was spending 60% of their time on administrative tasks rather than strategic work. They were turning away qualified leads because they simply couldn't scale their operations.

Critical Pain Points

  • • 23 emails on average per client onboarding
  • • 12+ hours of manual data entry per client
  • • Frequent errors in account setup causing delays
  • • No standardized process across team members
  • • Client frustration with lengthy onboarding

The Solution

We built an end-to-end automated onboarding system that guides clients through a smart intake process, automatically provisions accounts, and sets up all necessary integrations—all while keeping the team informed at every step.
