FinServe — AI Compliance Document Processing

Enterprise AI system processing 1,200+ compliance documents monthly with 95% faster turnaround and 99.7% accuracy

The Challenge

FinServe, a financial services compliance firm, was drowning in paperwork. Their team of 12 compliance specialists manually reviewed 1,200+ regulatory documents each month—KYC forms, AML reports, transaction records, and audit documents. Each document required 45-90 minutes of careful review, data extraction, and entry into their compliance management system.

The manual process was creating serious business risks:

  • 3-5 day processing time causing client delays and penalties
  • 8-12% error rate in manual data entry leading to compliance issues
  • Team burnout from repetitive, high-stakes work
  • Unable to scale without hiring (each hire = $85K+ annually)
  • No audit trail or version control on document processing

The Solution

We built an enterprise-grade AI document processing system that combines OCR, natural language processing, and machine learning to automatically extract, validate, and categorize compliance data with human-level accuracy.

AI Processing Pipeline

1
Document Ingestion

2
OCR & Text Extraction
Google Cloud Vision extracts text with 99.9% accuracy
3
Document Classification
AI categorizes document type (KYC, AML, Transaction, Audit, etc.)
4
Data Extraction
GPT-4 extracts 50+ structured data points per document
5
Validation & Compliance Check
Automated verification against regulatory requirements
6
Human Review (if needed)
Low-confidence items flagged for specialist review
7
Database Integration
Structured data synced to compliance management system

  • Complete audit trail with timestamp and user tracking
  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • GDPR and CCPA compliant data handling
  • Automated data retention and deletion policies
