Production-ready AI SaaS platform with 7 content tools, Stripe payments, and user dashboard built in 6 weeks for 85% less cost

The Challenge

A content marketing agency wanted to launch their own AI-powered SaaS platform to serve their clients directly, but traditional development quotes were coming in at $180K-$250K with 9-12 month timelines. They needed a faster, more cost-effective solution without sacrificing quality or features.

Key requirements included:

  • 7 AI-powered content generation tools (blog posts, social media, emails, etc.)
  • Stripe subscription payments with 3 pricing tiers
  • User authentication and personalized dashboards
  • Content history and export functionality
  • Mobile-responsive design with modern UI

The Solution

We built a production-ready MVP using modern no-code and low-code tools, integrating best-in-class AI APIs, and delivering a polished product in just 6 weeks at a fraction of traditional development costs.
