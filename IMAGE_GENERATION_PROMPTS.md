## Image Specifications

- **Resolution**: Minimum 2400x1600px for hero banners, 1920x1080px for screenshots
- **Format**: PNG with transparency where applicable
- **Color Profile**: sRGB
- **Style**: Photorealistic product photography or crisp UI screenshots
- **Background**: Pure white (#FFFFFF) or subtle gradients
- **Lighting**: Soft, natural lighting for product shots
- **Shadows**: Subtle, realistic shadows for depth
- **Text**: Crisp, readable at all sizes
- **UI Elements**: Sharp, high-contrast, professional

---

## Usage Notes

1. Generate hero banner images first for immediate use
2. Create detail screenshots to populate case study pages
3. Ensure consistency in style across all images for each case study
4. Use glassmorphism sparingly and tastefully
5. Maintain Apple-inspired minimal aesthetic throughout
6. Prioritize clarity and professionalism over decorative elements
