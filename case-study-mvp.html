Rapid MVP

Startup MVP — Launch in 3 Weeks

Production-ready fitness coaching platform that secured $500K seed funding and acquired 1,000 users in the first month

The Challenge

A first-time founder with a vision for an AI-powered fitness coaching platform needed to validate their idea quickly and affordably. They had $15K in savings and 6 weeks before their lease ended—meaning they needed to either secure funding or return to their corporate job.

Traditional development agencies quoted $80K-$120K with 4-6 month timelines. Hiring a technical co-founder would mean giving up 30-40% equity. They needed a different approach.

Startup Requirements

  • • AI-powered workout plan generator based on user goals
  • • User profiles with progress tracking and photos
  • • Video library with 100+ exercise demonstrations
  • • In-app messaging with certified coaches
  • • Subscription payments ($29/month)
  • • Mobile-responsive design (mobile-first)
  • • Must launch within 3-4 weeks to meet investor demo deadline

The Solution

We used a strategic MVP approach: build only what's essential to validate the core value proposition, use proven tools and frameworks, and prioritize speed without sacrificing quality. The result was a production-ready platform in just 21 days.

3-Week Development Sprint

Week 1: Foundation
  • • Firebase setup (auth, Firestore, storage)
  • • User registration & login flows
  • • Basic user profile creation
  • • Stripe integration for subscriptions
  • • Responsive layout framework
Week 2: Core Features
  • • AI workout generator (GPT-4 integration)
  • • Exercise video library (100+ videos)
  • • Progress tracking dashboard
  • • Photo upload & comparison tool
  • • In-app messaging system
Week 3: Polish & Launch
  • • Mobile optimization & testing
  • • Onboarding flow refinement
  • • Email notifications setup
  • • Performance optimization
  • • Beta user testing & bug fixes
  • • Production deployment

Smart MVP Decisions

What We Built

AI workout generator, progress tracking, video library, messaging, payments

What We Deferred (v2)

Social features, nutrition tracking, wearable integrations, native mobile apps

Key Insight

Focus on ONE core value prop: AI-generated personalized workouts. Everything else supports this.
